**Title:**  
In-Vehicle Cloud Storage for Personal Data and Music  

---

**Goal:**  
The In-Vehicle Cloud Storage feature will allow users to store, access, and manage their personal data, preferences, and music collections within the vehicle's cloud-connected system. This storage will be accessible from any compatible luxury electric vehicle within the brand and will enable a personalized, seamless experience, including automatically recalling user settings, music playlists, and other preferences. This feature aims to enhance the in-vehicle experience by providing convenience, personalization, and security for the user's digital content.  

---

**Preconditions:**  
1. The vehicle must be equipped with a compatible cloud connection module and In-Vehicle Cloud Storage capabilities.
2. Users, such as Alise or Bob, should have a registered account within the vehicle's system, which is linked to the cloud storage.
3. The vehicle should be parked or connected to a stable internet connection to initially sync or update the user’s data in the cloud.

---

**Main Scenario:**  
1. **User Action:** Alise enters the vehicle and logs in to her user profile.
   - **Sub-System Response:** The In-Vehicle Cloud Storage syncs her personal data, preferences, and music playlists from the cloud.
  
2. **User Action:** Alise selects a music playlist on the infotainment screen.
   - **Sub-System Response:** The system retrieves the playlist from cloud storage and begins playback.

3. **User Action:** Alise adjusts seat position, climate control, and mirror settings to her preference.
   - **Sub-System Response:** The system saves these adjustments in the cloud under her profile for future sessions.

4. **User Action:** Alise adds a new song to her "Favorites" playlist in the vehicle.
   - **Sub-System Response:** The song is uploaded and saved to the cloud, updating her playlist across all compatible devices.

5. **User Action:** Alise finishes her drive and exits the vehicle.
   - **Sub-System Response:** The system automatically syncs any changes made during the drive, ensuring her data and settings are updated in the cloud.

---

**Alternative Scenario:**  
1. **User Action:** Bob attempts to access his music playlist in the vehicle, but the cloud connection is temporarily unavailable.
   - **Sub-System Response:** The system notifies Bob of the lack of connectivity and offers to play locally saved music, if available.

2. **User Action:** Bob decides to continue listening to his previously downloaded playlist.
   - **Sub-System Response:** The system retrieves the last synced version of the playlist and begins playback, displaying a notification that any changes made will be synced once the connection is restored.

3. **User Action:** Bob makes changes to his seat settings and saves them.
   - **Sub-System Response:** The system saves the settings locally and queues them for cloud synchronization when connectivity is restored.

---

**Postconditions:**  
After successfully interacting with the In-Vehicle Cloud Storage system, the user’s personal settings, playlists, and any other adjustments are securely stored and accessible across all compatible luxury electric vehicles. These settings will automatically be available upon the user’s next login, ensuring a consistent, personalized experience.
